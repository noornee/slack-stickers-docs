## Stickers for Slack

Welcome to Stickers for Slack, where boring text messages go to die and fun reigns supreme! Ever wished you could drop the perfect reaction sticker in Slack without hunting for it on the internet? Well, wish granted! 🪄 With Stickers for Slack, you can send stickers effortlessly and keep your Slack convos spicy. 🌶️ Whether you're hyping up a teammate, dropping a savage comeback, or just need the perfect GIF to say "I told you so," we've got you covered. 🎯

With this bot, you can:
✅ Search for GIFs using **slash commands** (`/sticker <search query>`)
✅ Use **mentions** (`@sticker gif <search query>`) to search within conversations
✅ Preview GIFs privately before sharing
✅ Shuffle through different GIFs until you find the perfect one
✅ Send GIFs in both **channels and direct messages**

The bot is designed to be lightweight, intuitive, and fun to use while keeping Slack conversations engaging.
